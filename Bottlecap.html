Bottlecap Building

A collection of creative and engineering projects centered around bottle caps — from functional builds to artistic designs. Each project reflects my hands-on approach and knack for turning everyday objects into something unique.

Bottlecap Gun

This project explores the playful side of engineering by transforming bottle caps into safe projectiles. Using household materials, I designed and built a simple bottlecap launcher that demonstrates basic mechanics and creativity. It's a fun experiment in upcycling and hands-on tinkering.

Door Table

I took an old door off of its hinges and was struck with the strange urge to turn it into a board gaming table. I found some scattered pieces around the house and got to work. This project took about 10 hours to complete with non stop gluing, sanding, and resining bottlecaps onto the table. The end result is a unique and functional piece of furniture that adds character to my room.
