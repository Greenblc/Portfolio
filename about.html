Hey, I’m Chris.

I’m a software engineer who loves turning ideas into reality. Whether I’m wiring LEDs on a Raspberry Pi, optimizing a React app, or writing MIPS assembly for a maze game, I dive in headfirst and learn as I go.

My career has taken me from IT support and field tech roles—where I debugged networks and installed hardware—to collaborative dev teams using modern JavaScript stacks. I’m equally comfortable soldering circuits and crafting user interfaces with React or vanilla JS.

Right now I’m wrapping up my software engineering degree, job hunting for front‑end or full‑stack roles, and polishing personal projects. Lately I’ve been experimenting with Unreal Engine in C++, building embedded systems, and growing a portfolio that shows both my structured engineer side and my curious tinkerer side.

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me skateboarding, rock climbing, or buried in manga (can’t resist a Strawhat adventure). My cat Java insists on supervising every late‑night project, so most code reviews include a purr of approval.

On this site you’ll find:

  • Personal projects and demos I’ve built
  • Blog posts on tech experiments and learnings
  • An evolving portfolio that reflects where I’m headed next

Thanks for stopping by! If you want to collaborate or just chat about tech (or manga), feel free to reach out.
