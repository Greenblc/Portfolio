The Game Dev Spiral

Started a 3D elemental platformer around February 2025. The goal? Let the player control two of four elements—fire, water, air, and earth. The reality? 30+ hours of tweaking jump arcs, breaking Unity prefabs, and wondering if particles are worth my sanity. I just wanted to make a cool little game. Now I’m knee-deep in physics bugs and design choices that keep evolving every time I “finish” them. Will it be done soon? Define “soon.”
